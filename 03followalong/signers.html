who were the signers?

famous signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

John Hancock
Famous for his large, bold signature, he was the President of the Continental Congress.
Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration and later the 3rd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned statesman, inventor, and diplomat.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence and the 2nd President of the United States.
Samuel Adams
A key organizer of the Boston Tea Party and influential in the independence movement.
